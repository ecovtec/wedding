Tell us about your betrothed!

Christopher William

Christopher is the most wonderful, handsome, and charming man I know. He is thoughtful, selfless, and has the sweetest servant's heart. He steps in to meet the needs of others even when he is not asked to do so. What I love most about him is the way his love for God manifests itself in how he constantly cares for others and puts them first. Not to mention he has a brilliant mind and is crazy talented at running!

Angela Megan

Angie is the kind of beautiful that makes you turn your head when you notice her walk into a room. Then the first time I heard her speak she hit me with a depth and genuineness that completely caught me off guard. I was struck by the genuineness of her faith when she wanted to know about dinosaurs and how they work with the Bible. The way that she cares deeply and truly for everyone she meets encourages and challenges me every day. I've never met someone who is so fun and easy to spend an entire day with. We could have incredible deep conversation, or do nothing exceptional at all, and it would still be a perfect day.
